🧭 How to Play

You’ll face three kinds of “runes”:
  • Compare: pick >, <, or =
  • Order: pick the correct sorted list
  • Round: round to nearest 10 / 100 / 1,000 / 10,000
Scoring:
  • Correct: +10 base
  • Streak bonus: +2 per streak (caps at +20)
  • Speed bonus: up to +10
  • Wrong: lose 1 life, streak resets

🧙 Parent Mini-Guide

If a round is missed, ask:
  • “What digit is in the rounding place?”
  • “What digit is right after it?”
  • “5 or more: round up, otherwise down.”
For ordering, suggest comparing the largest place first (ten-thousands, thousands, hundreds…).
